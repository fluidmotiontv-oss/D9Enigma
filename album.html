🧬 WHAT IS A SOVEREIGN NODE?

A Sovereign Node is a single, self-contained HTML file containing your entire audio-visual album player, visualizers, and links. It runs anywhere, fully offline, and is downloadable by fans as a direct file.

💰 PLATFORM FEES & CO-OP SPLITS

Payments route peer-to-peer directly from listeners to you (via PayPal, Venmo, or crypto addresses).
INTRO WAVE POLICY: The 1% platform commission is waived for your first 54 sold copies (100% direct artist support). From copy 55 onward, a tiny 1% split routes to Timothy Dwen / Dragon 9 Co-op for offline PWA framework maintenance.

🌀 DRAGON 9 TIME RESOLUTION

Decoupled from standard commercial timetables, Dragon 9 tracks listener time in 54-minute hours. Fans earn H-Notes as they listen, which they can use to unlock locked tracks or download stems.

🚀 STEP-BY-STEP PUBLISHING GUIDE
  1. 1. Onboard tracks, upload cover image URLs, custom links, or stems ZIP.
  2. 2. Enter your PayPal email or crypto address in the Payment fields.
  3. 3. Click "MINT & SAVE CONFIGURATION" to write configuration parameters.
  4. 4. Click "DOWNLOAD STANDALONE HTML" to compile your standalone player.
  5. 5. Check "Publish to Cooperative Shop" and enter your cloud URL (e.g. GitHub Pages or GCS static link) so listeners can find your node in the global registry!
