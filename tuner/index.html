How to Tune Your Instrument

Tuning by Ear (Generator): Click any guitar string preset (E2 to E4) to generate a reference pitch tone. Match your guitar's harmonic pitch to the generator output. You can use the slider to adjust the pitch to custom values or sweep target Solfeggio frequencies.

Interactive Tuning (Reader): Click the Microphone button and grant input permissions. Pluck a string. The frequency reader analyzes your micro-signals and highlights the nearest pitch. Watch the dial needle: adjust string tension until the needle aligns at exactly 0 cents (turning the note readout green).
