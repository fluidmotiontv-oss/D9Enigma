Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

Lotus Nexus values and asserts your right to civil liberties, offline privacy, and self-determination. This Privacy Policy details how data is managed within the Lotus Nexus application.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, monitor, track, store, or transmit any of your personal identification data, location details, metadata, or creative compositions. Lotus Nexus is a decentralized, standalone application.

2. Local Execution and Storage

All processes related to synthesis, loop playback, and composition generation run entirely on your local hardware:

  • Resonance Codes: Generated Base64 text codes are computed client-side using JavaScript, and are only copied to your device's clipboard at your explicit action. They are never sent to external servers.
  • Local Library: Custom compositions saved to your library are stored locally in your browser's localStorage or IndexedDB. Clearing your browser data will delete these files. We cannot access them.

3. Zero Third-Party Integrations

Lotus Nexus does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics scripts, social tracking tools, or external telemetry modules.

4. Standalone Portability

Because the app is built to execute offline without server communication, you can safely run it inside a closed network or download the standalone HTML client directly to your local drive for complete air-gapped security.

5. Contact

Since no backend accounts exist, there is no support dashboard. For questions regarding the code, inspect the public application source directly.
